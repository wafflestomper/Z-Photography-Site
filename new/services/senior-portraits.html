Senior Portrait Sessions

Your senior year is a milestone worth celebrating. I specialize in creating senior portraits that capture your unique personality, interests, and achievements. Whether you're looking for traditional portraits or something more creative, I'll work with you to create images you'll treasure for years to come.

What to Expect

  • Personalized session planning
  • Multiple location options
  • Outfit changes included
  • Professional editing and retouching

Session Details

Senior portrait sessions typically last 1-2 hours and include multiple outfit changes. We can shoot at your school, a local park, or any location that's meaningful to you. All sessions include professional editing and retouching of your final images.
